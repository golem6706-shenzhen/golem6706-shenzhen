About Us

Team 6706 Golem is a FIRST Robotics Competition team from Shenzhen, China. Founded in 2017, we are dedicated to promoting STEM education and fostering innovation in robotics.

Our Achievements

  • 2022 Southern Cross Regional - Finalist
  • 2019 Shenzhen Regional - Winner
  • 2018 Southern Cross Regional - Rookie All-Star Award
