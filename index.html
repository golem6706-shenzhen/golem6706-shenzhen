About Us

Team 6706 Golem is a FIRST Robotics Competition team from Shenzhen, China. Founded in 2017, we are dedicated to promoting STEM education and fostering innovation in robotics.

Our Achievements

  • 2024 Won Innovation in Control Award in Iowa regional
  • 2024 top1 in the qualification of Beijing offseason
  • 2018 Won Quality Award in Shenzhen Regional - Winner
  • 2017 Greater Pittsburgh Regional - Highest Rookie Seed & Rookie Inspiration Award
